Coq au vin

A beautiful musty dish

This is a beautiful luxorious and musty dish to satisfy all your flavourbuds.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Chicken thighs
  • Onion and garlic
  • Cognac
  • Red wine
  • Chicken broth
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh thyme sprigs and bay leaf
  • Carrots and cremini mushrooms
  • Butter and all-purpose fluor

Steps

  1. Fry the coq
  2. Pour the wine
  3. Plate the pot
